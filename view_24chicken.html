24 Chicken is a popular food brand in the Philippines known for its delectable Korean-style fried chicken. They specialize in boneless chicken offerings, available in various flavors such as Original, Yangnyeom, Snow Cheese, and Jack Daniels. Customers can enjoy these flavors in different serving sizes, including half and whole orders, as well as rice meals. Additionally, 24 Chicken offers side dishes like Cajun Fries and Kimchi Rice to complement their main dishes.
